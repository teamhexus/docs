Creating a demo showcase page is a powerful way to showcase your strengths, engage your audience, and achieve your objectives more effectively.

### Add any content type on your showcase page

Add videos, guides, blogs and other content types to show everything about your product. Hexus AI enables you to create a showcase page that can be personalized to your brandâ€™s identity.&#x20;

Here's how you can do it -&#x20;

* Open your web browser and go to the Hexus application.

* Click on showcases in the left panel.

* Click on 'Create Showcase' to begin setting up your showcase.

* Proceed by clicking the check box for all stories you want to put up on your showcase.

* You can select from all content formats - Text-based stories or guides or interactive demos.

* Click on if you want to 'Add External Link' to include an external link in your showcase.

* To make your showcase available to others, click 'Publish'.

* To view your showcase as it will appear to others, click 'Preview'.

* If you need to make any changes, click 'Edit' to modify your showcase.
