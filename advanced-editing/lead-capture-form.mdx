## 2. Viewing Form Submissions

### Viewing Leads in Hexus

You can view form submissions in the “Leads” tab in the Hexus app.

<img height="200" src="/images/view-leads-in-hexus.png" /> 

### Viewing Form Submissions in HubSpot

If you have connected with HubSpot, every time someone submits a form, a contact is automatically created in HubSpot. More on HubSpot integration [here](/collaboration/integrations).

<img height="200" src="/images/view-hexus-leads-in-hubspot.png" /> 

## 3. Receiving notifications through email
