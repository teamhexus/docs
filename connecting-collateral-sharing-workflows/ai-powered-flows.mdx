## Product Demos
Use the Hexus Chrome Extension to record your product screens and convert them into interactive product demos.

You can also upload an existing product demo video to convert it into an interactive one or make any other changes and edits. AI Video to Demo conversion works best with screen recordings of digital products.

<img height="400" width="800" src="/images/ai-studio-1.png" />

## Email Follow Up
Hexus uses any previous demos you have created or uploaded to generate emails so you can follow up with customers with added, personalized context.
